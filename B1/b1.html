Wheel Types

Ackermann Steering Mode is highly similar to steering a car, turning the inner and outer wheels at the appropriate angles for more stable steering. This system is customizable based on the steering speed.

Tracked Steering Mode uses wide contact with the ground and adjusts steering speeds of the left and right sides to traverse multiple surfaces such as smooth roads, sandy and rugged roads, slopes, etc.

Mecanum Wheel Steering Mode can perform translation and rotation in place in any direction, including straight, oblique, horizontal, S-shaped travel, zero-radius rotation, narrow and irregular spaces at any angle, etc.
