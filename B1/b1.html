Extreme durability and resistance to external influence as well as the capability to work autonomously make Unitree B1 an indispensable tool for any activity implying harsh operating conditions.

Great for emergency service. Due to its maximum load capacity of 40 kg, the B1 is capable of cargo transportation over rough terrain to hard-to-reach places. It is perfectly suited for security patrol and inspection of hazardous environments.
